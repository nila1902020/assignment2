About Foxes

Foxes are small to medium-sized mammals known for their bushy tails and pointed ears. They belong to the family Canidae, which also includes wolves, dogs, and other similar animals.

Habitat

Foxes are found in a variety of habitats, including forests, grasslands, and urban areas. They are highly adaptable animals and have successfully adapted to living near humans in many parts of the world.

Diet

Foxes are omnivores, meaning they eat both plant and animal matter. Their diet includes small mammals, birds, insects, fruits, and even some carrion.
